Mandatory Equipment for Every Hiking Trip

The following is the list of all the equipment that you shold take with you on hiking trips in Montenegro. Please take this list seriously as all the items have been carefully selected by experienced hikers.

  1. Four liters of water per person
  2. Fresh shirts and socks
  3. Fruit bars

Places to Visit

  • Mountain Lodge "Za Vratlom"
  • Subra
  • Lovćen

The Mountain Lodge

The best way to reach the mountain lodge is to drive from Herceg-Novi to the now abandoned Hotel Borići. From there follow the gravel road which will lead you to the lodge. PK Subra Facebook Link

Subra

To reach the top peak of the Orijen mountain range, hike North-East from the mountain lodge. Once you reach a forest, turn north and follow the hiking trail.

Lovćen

From the city of Kotor go to the Austro-Hungarian fortress. From there follow the m shaped trail until you reach the main road. From there on follow the main hiking trail.
